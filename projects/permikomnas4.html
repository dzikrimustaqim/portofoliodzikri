The National Informatics and Computer Student Association, also abbreviated as "PERMIKOMNAS", was established from the National Informatics and Computer Engineering Student Association Meeting which was held on 19 - 21 August 2002 at PUSPIPTEK Serpong Tangerang and received an SK DIKTI in 2003. PERMIKOMNAS which consists of Student Associations of Informatics and Computer Universities throughout Indonesia who have activities in the scientific field in improving abilities and expertise while in college to assist universities in activities on and off campus.
